Recipe

Ingredients::

**For Puri:**

- 1 cup semolina (sooji)
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- Water (as needed)
- Oil (for frying)

**For Pani (Spiced Water):**

- 1 cup mint leaves
- 1/2 cup coriander leaves
- 1-2 green chilies
- 1-inch ginger
- 2 tablespoons tamari,nd pulp
- 1 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
- 1 teaspoon chaat masala
- Salt (to taste)
- Water (as needed)

**For Filling:**

- 1 cup boiled chickpeas or potatoes (mashed)
- 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala
- Salt (to taste)

Instructions:

**Make Puris**:

- Mix semolina, all-purpose flour, and water to form a stiff dough.
- Roll out small puris and deep fry until golden and puffed up. Set aside.

**Prepare Pani (Spiced Water)**:

- Blend mint, coriander, green chilies, ginger, and tamarind pulp with water.
- Strain and mix with cumin powder, chaat masala, salt, and additional water. Chill.

**Prepare Filling**:

- Mix boiled chickpeas or potatoes with chaat masala and salt.

**Assemble**:

- Crack the top of each puri, add the filling, and pour in the chilled pani. Enjoy immediately!

Tips:

- Serve immediately after filling the puris to keep them crispy.
- Adjust the spice and tanginess of the pani to your taste.
